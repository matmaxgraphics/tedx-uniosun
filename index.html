Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between TED and TEDx?

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and spread “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx events are organized independently under a free license granted by TED, allowing communities like ours at Uniosun to host their own local, self-organized events.

When and where is TEDxUniosun 2025?

The event is scheduled for the 29th of November, 2025. It will be a hybrid event, allowing for both in-person attendance at Uniosun and virtual participation. More details on the specific venue will be shared with ticket holders.

How can I get tickets?

You can secure your ticket by visiting our official ticketing page. Click the "Get Tickets" button at the top of this page or follow this link: bit.ly/tedxuniosun2025.

How can I become a sponsor or partner?

We are always looking for partners who share our passion for ideas worth spreading! If your organization is interested in sponsoring TEDxUniosun, please click the "Become a Sponsor" button at the top of the page or reach out to us through our social media channels.

How are the speakers chosen?

Our speakers are selected through a careful curation process based on our event's theme. We look for individuals with unique ideas, compelling stories, and a deep connection to the community. Speaker applications (when open) will be announced on our social media platforms.
